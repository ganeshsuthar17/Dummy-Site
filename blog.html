World Trends

What’s trending in skincare worldwide

Barrier-friendly formulas, minimalist routines, and skin tint SPF are leading the global conversation this year.

A simple skincare routine that works

  • Morning: Gentle cleanser, hydrating serum, moisturizer, and broad-spectrum SPF.
  • Evening: Cleanse, treat with a targeted serum, seal with moisturizer, and use retinol a few nights a week.
  • Weekly: Add a soothing mask or exfoliation only when your skin barrier feels comfortable.

Trending skincare topics worldwide

Barrier repair: People are choosing gentle, fragrance-free products that support the skin barrier.
Minimal skincare: Fewer products with better ingredients are becoming the go-to routine.
Daily SPF: Sunscreen remains one of the most popular skincare essentials globally.
Glow-focused care: Hydration, niacinamide, and vitamin C are still leading the way for healthy radiance.
